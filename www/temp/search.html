#1 Servers taking too long to load.

Issue:

If the servers take an abnormally amount of time to load, the issue is you either cleared you cookies from the runners, or you're not binded to the runners.

Solution:

You need to reconnect to the servers. You can do that by pressing a button below that you think can help you.
